# User Guide: AI CV Generator with Career Matching

## Overview

This application generates a professional CV based on the data you provide and evaluates the CV's suitability for a target job. The evaluation is based on a career matching rubric.

## Steps to Use the Application

1. Open the Application

Run the following command to launch the application:

```bash```
streamlit run pages/form.py


2. Fill Out the Form
Once the application is open, fill out the following fields:

Full Name: Your name.

Professional Experience: Enter your job history and responsibilities.

Education History: Add details of your educational background.

Skills / Certifications: List your key skills and certifications (comma-separated).

Target Job Description: Paste the job description for the role you're targeting.
3. Submit the Form
Click the "Generate CV" button. The system will process the data and generate your CV. It will also provide an evaluation report based on your CVâ€™s suitability for the target job.

4. View Your CV and Evaluation
Generated CV: The app will display your formatted CV.

Career Match Evaluation: The app will provide a career match evaluation, including a detailed scoring rubric based on the job description.
